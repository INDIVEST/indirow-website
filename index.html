About Us

  • Indirow is a fintech platform for seamless and easy retail loan facilitation for MSME and Salaried customers and is promoted by Internovo Ventures, a company that is privately owned by leading financial and technology sector entrepreneurs.
  • It is backed by Interface Ventures, which is the family office of Karan Desai, a serial entrepreneur in financial services and healthcare and Akshay Srivastava, the promoter of a new-age technology development company Novostack.
  • Karan has over 16 years of experience across the financial services domain, professionally as an investment banker and most recently having co-founded and built a large, BSE listed retail NBFC
  • Akshay is a seasoned tech entrepreneur who runs his boutique tech firm NovoStack, based out of Noida. He is a serial entrepreneur having built & sold his first start-up while in college and specialises in technology for financial services.

Partner with Us

  • Indirow welcomes partnerships with businesses that offer services, other than access to credit to their customers. We aim to fill this gap in the partner’s portfolio and offer loan facilitation services to our partners’ customers, taking full responsibility for servicing the client ourselves.

  • This helps ensure that our partners’ clients have seamless access to the credit that they need for their business or personal purposes from top quality lenders. Partners can track progress of their client cases via their realtime dashboard.

  • On the distribution side, Indirow has strategic collaborations with financial institutions that facilitate over US$ 3 billion of retail loans disbursements on an annual basis.

  • Experian, a global leader in credit reporting is our credit bureau partner.
