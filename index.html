About us

  • Indirow is a fintech platform for seamless and easy retail loan facilitation for MSME and Salaried customers and is promoted by Internovo Ventures, a company that is privately owned by leading financial and technology sector entrepreneurs.
  • It is backed by Interface Ventures, which is the family office of Karan Desai, a serial entrepreneur in financial services and healthcare and Akshay Srivastava, the promoter of a new-age technology development company Novostack.
  • Karan has over 16 years of experience across the financial services domain, professionally as an investment banker and most recently having co-founded and built a large, Bombay Stock Exchange listed retail NBFC, which he continues to remain a shareholder of. He also serves as a Director on the Board of RULoans, which is a multi-billion dollar, financial products distribution platform based in India and the UAE.
  • Akshay is a seasoned tech entrepreneur who runs his boutique tech firm NovoStack, based out of Noida. He is a serial entrepreneur having built & sold his first start-up while in college and specialises in technology for financial services.
